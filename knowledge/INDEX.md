# Knowledge Layer Index (General)

Domain folders within `knowledge/`, tracked in git. This is for product management learning that generalises beyond any single company: frameworks, patterns, and observations that hold regardless of employer or product.

Do not put company names, product names, customer names, or other identifying detail here. Anything tied to a specific company or product belongs in `product-context/memory/` instead, which is gitignored.

Each domain folder contains:
- `KNOWLEDGE.md` — confirmed facts and patterns
- `HYPOTHESES.md` — observations needing more data, with confidence ratings
- `RULES.md` — confirmed patterns to apply by default

When a hypothesis is confirmed by 3+ independent instances, promote it to a rule. When a rule is contradicted by new data, demote it back to a hypothesis.

Copy `template/` when starting a new domain folder.
